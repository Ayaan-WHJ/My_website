Indoor Games





Indoor Treasure Hunt

Hide things around the house for your children to find.



Paper Plane Launcher

Make a paper plane and launch it. Try to make the best plane you can.



Plastic Bottle Bowling

Use a tennis ball and some empty water or soda bottles to make a bowling lane.



Balloon Badminton

Use paper plates and balloons to turn your living room into a badminton court.



Mini Golf Course

Create festive flags and be creative with indoor obstacles. Use tin cans for the holes and cardboard for the club.



Tornado in a Jar

Using a clear, round bottle , have the kids fill it with water and dish soap. Then let them shake it to make a tornado.
